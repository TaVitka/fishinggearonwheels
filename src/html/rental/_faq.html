Frequently asked question

  • What types of fishing equipment sets are available for rent?

    We offer a variety of fishing equipment sets suitable for different types of fishing, including rods, reels, tackle, and other necessary accessories

  • How can I reserve fishing equipment?

    You can reserve the desired equipment set online on our website

  • What are the rental terms and costs?

    Rental terms include the specified rental period and deposit for certain equipment sets. The rental cost depends on the selected set and rental period

  • Can I extend the rental period of the equipment?

    The possibility of extending the rental period depends on the availability of equipment sets. Please contact us to request an extension

  • How does payment and deposit work?

    Payment is made upon receiving the equipment set. The deposit can be paid in cash or by credit card and will be refunded upon return of the equipment set in good condition

  • What should I do if the equipment is damaged or lost?

    Please contact us immediately to discuss the next steps. Depending on the circumstances, a damage fee or equipment replacement may apply
