How it works

  • Equipment selection

    Browse our catalog of high-quality fishing gear and select the items you need for your fishing trip

  • Reservation

    Fill in all the necessary information in the booking form

  • Order confirmation

    After submitting your reservation request, our staff will contact you
    to confirm the order details and equipment availability

  • Payment and documents

    Upon order confirmation, you will receive payment instructions.
    We may also request necessary documents (e.g., ID or deposit) depending on requirements

  • Equipment pickup

    Arrive at the designated location and time to collect your rented equipment. Our staff will assist you with equipment inspection and formalities

  • Equipment use

    Enjoy fishing with our quality equipment throughout the rental period

  • Equipment return

    Return the equipment at the agreed time and place after the rental period ends. We will inspect the equipment's condition and return any documents or deposits provided
